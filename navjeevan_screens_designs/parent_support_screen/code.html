Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the post-adoption follow up last? expand_more
Post-adoption follow-up visits are typically conducted every six months for a period of two years after the legal adoption is finalized.
What documents are needed for registration? expand_more
Key documents include PAN card, birth certificates, proof of residence, health certificates, and income tax returns for the last three years.
Can I update my preferences after registration? expand_more
Yes, certain preferences can be updated through the CARINGS portal, but some changes might require a re-evaluation of the Home Study Report.
