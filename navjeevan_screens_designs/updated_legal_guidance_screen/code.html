Understanding the CARA Process in Pune

A clear, legal roadmap to bringing your child home with love and security.

verified CARA Aligned
lock Safe & Secure
1

Registration on CARA Portal

Start your journey by registering online on the official CARA portal. We recommend having your basic identity and residence proofs ready for a smooth start.

2

Document Verification by NGO

A dedicated local NGO official in Pune will assist you in verifying your submitted documents to ensure every legal requirement is met with care.

3

Home Study Report (HSR)

A friendly social worker will visit your home to understand your environment and help prepare your family for the beautiful addition of a child.

4

Child Matching & Referral

Based on your profile and legal eligibility, CARA will provide child referrals. You'll have the opportunity to see photos and medical reports.

5

Legal Pre-Adoption Placement

The final step where the child joins your home under a legal placement. Our legal experts will guide you through the final court order process.

"Every child deserves a loving home. We are here to help you through the journey."
